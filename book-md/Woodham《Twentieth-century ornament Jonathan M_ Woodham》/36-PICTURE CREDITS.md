Jonathan Woodham was born in Glasgow in 1950. He is Principal Lecturer in Design History at Brighton Polytechnic and a member of the Editorial Board of the Journal of Design History. He is also a member of the CNAA register of Specialist Advisers and of the Hong Kong Provisional Council for Academic Accreditation. Author of The Industrial Designer and the Public (1983), Jonathan Woodham has contributed to: Design and Industry (1980), Design Since 1945 (1985), Did Britain Make It? (1986), Design History: a Student Handbook (1987) and Tradizione e Modernismo: Design 1918-40 (1988). He contributes to several journals including the Journal of Design History, Art History, Design and Designer. He lives in Brighton with his wife and son.
